dominant strategy


For any player i, a strategy s*∈Si weakly dominates another strategy s′∈Si if:

∀s-i∈S-i[ui(s*,s-i)≥ui(s′,s-i)]

(Remember that S-i represents the product of all strategy sets other than i’s)

s* strongly dominates s′ if:

∀s-i∈S-i[ui(s*,s-i)>ui(s′,s-i)]
